Y U T B

YUTB is a social media content creation tool based on YouTube footage. The operation of the apppcation is based on technologies developed by OpenAI and consists of three main stages:


- Creation of a transcription based on the audio extracted from the video

- A note about the film is created on the basis of the transcription

- The final stage is the generation of social media content based on the information contained in the note


If you want to learn more about the technical aspects of my apppcation, take a look at its repositories on GitHub.

You are welcome to see examples of generated content on the dashboard, and to try out the app on your own film (pnkedIn account required).
